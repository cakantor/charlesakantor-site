biography

Charles Kantor is a French AI entrepreneur, futurist, and Founder of H Company (Holistic AI), the Paris-based startup that raised $220M from Eric Schmidt, Yuri Milner, Xavier Niel, Bernard Arnault, Amazon, Samsung, Accel, among others, and pioneered "action-oriented" AI agents. Dubbed "the French Sam Altman" by Le Point magazine and praised by President Emmanuel Macron, Kantor is a leading voice in the race towards Superintelligence and European AI innovation, focusing on agentic AI systems and AGI infrastructure.
