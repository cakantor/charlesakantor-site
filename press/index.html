biography

Charles Kantor is a French AI entrepreneur and founding CEO of H Company, the Paris-based startup that raised €220 m and pioneers “action-oriented” AI agents for enterprise automation. Dubbed “the French Sam Altman” by Le Point magazine and praised by President Macron, Kantor is a leading voice in European AI innovation focusing on agentic AI systems and artificial general intelligence.
